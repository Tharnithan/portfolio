TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PHOTO:
===============================================

STEP 1: Find or Take Your Photo
--------------------------------
- Use a professional headshot
- Clear face, good lighting
- Clean background preferred
- Smiling, confident expression


STEP 2: Prepare Your Photo
---------------------------

Option A - Use Online Resizer (EASIEST):
1. Go to: https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image
2. Upload your photo
3. Resize to: Width 600, Height 600 pixels
4. Download the resized image


Option B - Use Canva (FREE):
1. Go to: https://www.canva.com
2. Click "Create a design"
3. Custom size: 600 x 600 pixels
4. Upload your photo
5. Fit to canvas
6. Download as JPG


Option C - Use Your Phone:
1. Take a square photo
2. Crop to square (1:1 ratio)
3. Send to your computer


STEP 3: Rename the File
------------------------
IMPORTANT: The file MUST be named exactly:
profile.jpg
